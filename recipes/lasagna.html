Lasagna

Description

Garfield's favorite dish, it's a guinea delicacy!

It's cheesy, saucy, and you can stuff it full of meat, or not.

Ingredients

  • Some o dem big flat-ass noodles
  • Ya favorite sauce
  • A few different Italian-ass cheeses
  • Meat, if you want it
  • Oil and spices and shit like that

Steps

  1. Getcha pan out
  2. Lay down some o dem noodles
  3. Pour in some of that saucy sauce
  4. Cheeese it up
  5. Meat (optional)
  6. Repeat
  7. Repeat again but no meat on top
  8. Bake at 350 until your house smells great and the edges just start to brown
